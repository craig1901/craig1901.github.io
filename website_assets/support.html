Phonetic Transcriber Support And Help

To use this app is really simple!

  • - Simply enter text into the form and press enter or the submit buttong to get the phonetic of a word.
  • - If you don't wanna type it, no problem! Just highlight a word from your document and the app will do it for you and insert the phonetic beside the word!
  • - You can also have our app speak the word back to you just to ensure you get a good grasp on the pronunciation!
